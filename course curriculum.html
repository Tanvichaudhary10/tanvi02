Special Characters (HTML Entities)

Some common HTML entities:

  • & — ampersand (write &amp;)
  • < — less-than (write &lt;)
  • > — greater-than (write &gt;)
  • © — copyright (write &copy;)
  • ® — registered (write &reg;)
  • € — euro sign (write &euro;)
  • ☃ — numeric entity (snowflake symbol)
  • – — en-dash and em-dash

Example sentence with special characters: © 2025 ACME Learning   · € 49 – limited offer.
